
OVERARCHING

~ 10,000 VL: Consentos births life into the world. The first Elves are created from his essence and begin to populate the land. Miasha is created solely after, remaining more of an affiliated, wayward spirit/presence guided by Consentos as a pet project.

~ 4000 VL: Consentos gives his own life up to grant Godhood to his favored daughter, Miasha. The sudden shock from his death releases immense amounts of Magic into the world.

~ 3600 VL: The first Elven tribals societies begin to harness Magic for the first time in the form of Mana.

~ 3200 VL: Miasha births the twins Voadn and Vinhema.

~ 600 VL: Voadn grows tired of his mother's favoritism towards his sister, creating Humanity to wreak havoc upon the Elven tribal realms and serve him. Humans begin to spread rapidly throughout the world, utilizing their ingenuity and resourcefulness to gain ground.

~ 300 VL: Elvendom and Humanity are all-but official enemies, battling each other in all realms, Wudor included.

~ 0 VL / VD: Voadn is slain by his sister. Elvendom is triumphant.

CONTEMPORARY

~ 15 VD: The remaining realms of Elvendom band together and form the great Shining Empire, a realm largely dominated by the overarching power of it's magical rulers, and run by families of Elven patriarchs.

~ 83 VD: The Great Shining Empire uncovers the Eternal Flame of Nacheviyan in Quzarat. Many begin to worship it for it's magical properties, becoming the first Drow.

~ 106 VD: An ecumenical council of mostly Elves is summoned to hear an auspice from the Gods, determining the Myriad to be the rightful successor 

~ 129 VD: Geffer suffers immense torture under his Orcish captors, sparing vital information that would have endangered the livelihood of the Shining Empire, and is granted Godhood by the Myriad for his selfless act.

~ 158 VD: Aengmir is conquered by the First Shining Empire after a decisive defeat of the [[Velm People]] and their clans. A lasting succession of conflicts between the FSE and Velmir begins.

~ 199 VD: Magic Addiction is discovered after a generation of Elves have worshipped the Eternal Flame, their skin turning pitch black. Regulations are introduced as lackluster efforts are put in place to find a cure.

~209 VD: The first attempt at creating a universal magical compendium 

~ 258-309 VD: Uenatos II "Chain-Holder" begins his reign, interfering with inheritance laws and allowing the breakup and splitting of estates on the holder's death.

~ 283 VD: Hteros joins the ranks of the Myriad. His worship begins prevelantly in Imperial territories within Quzarat.

~ 358 VD: The first Orcish Horde led by Gadash Feasts-upon-Bones is founded, confounding many of the states in the Greater and Lesser Koy.

~ 396 VD: Gadash creates a warlock pact with Jhul'zurjin, solidifying the loyalty of all Orckind to his enthrallment.

~ 445 VD: Gadash Feasts-upon-Bones subjugates most of the Koy, and begins raids into Imperial territories, most fairly easily repelled.

~ 499 VD: The nascent magical power within magic-addicted Elves is first recognized. 

~ 575 VD: The Shining Empire loses it's first conflict against Qayyid runic mages in Quzarat, sparking concerns about fading magical power. The Drow are subsequently made into living weapons for the Empire, known as the Kunosa'vlari, or Point of the Spear.

~ 603 VD: The Kunosa'vlari destroy Gadash Feasts-upon-Bones after his second attempt to destroy the Shining Empire, in which he made it as far as Teurecht.


~ 701 VD: The Siegebreaker Horde is founded among the warring Orcish clans, wreaking havoc in the far east and displacing many Koy and Valeni.

~ 728 VD: Elvish and Orcish realms drive the Valeni tribes out of their ancestral homeland in the far east. The remaining Valeni barbarians flee to Wudor, meeting an already declining Shining Empire.

~ 744 VD: The Valeni defeat the Shining Empire in Kalward, securing further expansion routes. Ettish settlers would resettle near the elven ruins, creating Galvar. The Shining Empire is geographically halved.

~ 788 VD: Valens is founded by Aucen de Fleur after uniting the disparate Valeni tribes.
