The Schildswarths (Ettish for Shield-Sworn) were the various Ettish nobility and gentry that passively resisted and subsequently rose up against the [[Kingdom of Valens]] during the pre-war period. They were enemies of the [[Valkoons]] and largely the predecessors of the [[Republic of Ettmark]]during the [[Second Ettish War]].
