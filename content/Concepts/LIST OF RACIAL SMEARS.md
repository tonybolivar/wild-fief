

AGAINST VALENICS.
**Valcoon:** Ettish for Falcon, used for Ettish polities who "slave for the Valenic Falcon"

AGAINST THE ETTISH.
**Mangeur/Boucmangeur** - A Valenic term for shield-eater, taken from the Ettish Shield-Sworn who vowed to remove Valenic tyranny from their lands.

**Eulenchen** - 'Little Owls,' used by both Teurechtis and Etts within the Shining Empire to refer to Ettish outside of it.

**Knife-ear:** A rather generic term.

**Treefucker:** Self explanatory.

**Stofkike / Stafkeik:** "Dust-kicked," in Ettish and Teurechti specifically, referring to their now-lost position as the dominant race of the world.
