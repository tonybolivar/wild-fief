Lindwyrms are a race of [[Wyrm]] borne from the failed attempt by the [[Astringent Order]] to purify the body of [[Linde ZuKalward]]. The failure of the ritual is largely considered the Astringent's failure to realize that she was one of many spawn of [[Vhaimos]] active in the world, though it is theorized that the Grandmaster of the [[Keislau]] cloister at the time was, in truth, a Druid in the [[Seething Eye]].
