The Invasion of Carettia was a series of military conflicts beginning in 1291 with the entry of [[Archebaud de Fleur]] and an army of 35,000 men into the Marquisate of Marziellana. Constituting the first instance of formal war between the [[Kingdom of Valens]] and the [[First Shining Empire]], the Onyx King's aims in this regard remain uncertain. The opening of a front in the southwest against the only other nation in Wudor whose military prowess matched that of Valens, in light of existing conflicts against the [[Evershining Empire]] and the [[Free Republic of Margard]], largely regarded as the spiritual precursor to the [[Republic of Ettmark]], is widely considered the beginning of the end for the former successes of the [[Onyx King's War]]. 

Later speculation raises the question of the [[Obscene Rite]], and the idea that Archebaud invaded [[Carettia]] in order to secure the means to perform it. As the core region of the [[First Shining Empire]], a large number of untouched magical artifacts and texts on pre-collapse rites were strewn about the country, with excavation slow due to the region's consistent conflict with the [[Evershining Elves]] to the east and the resistance of the Carettic people to the authority of the [[Second Shining Empire]], which despite it's claims to the contrary, was largely a Teurechti state claiming the mantle of an empire the Carettics considered their own.
