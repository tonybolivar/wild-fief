The Marquisate of Marziellana was the largest polity in [[Carettia]] and the spiritual leader of the [[Scazzelari]] during the [[Carettic Divorce]]. The DelaMarzi were known for their tactical use of precision archery to disrupt opposing magical formations, a tactic that they frequently utilized in their repeated conquests of smaller Carettic states, as well as their battles against the Drow.

Considered to possess the greatest martial prowess in the region, the Marziellani envied the dominance of the [[Teurechti People]], and in particular the [[ZeuDeulers]], over everyday life in Carettia.
