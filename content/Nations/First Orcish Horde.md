The **First Orcish Horde** was a polity between [[Wudor]] and Daelyug. When the Orcs were violently pushed out of Daelyug by the [[Hobgoblin]]s, they wandered the wastes for decades until [[Gadash Feasts-upon-Bones]] discovered the rich lands of the [[Koy People]], largely defenseless and spread across vast, unmanageable plains.

The Orcs would surge into Wudor in 355 AD, displacing the [[Auceni]] as they were forced to move westwards, and destroying the territories of the [[Zalokow Koy]].
