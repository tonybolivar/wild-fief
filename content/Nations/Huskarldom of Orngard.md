The Huskarldom of Orngard was a client state of the [[Realm of Velmir]] established during the [[Invasion of Reifenwald]]. The extent of it's claimed territories roughly corresponded with the Velmiric population in the Orngard peninsula proper.

> Demographics

During the events of the [[Invasion of Reifenwald]], Orngard and Reifenwald's combined population was around 800,000 people.
