> A Carettic Pirate.

Inhabitants of the "Birthplace of the Myriad" in the Ecumenicum, the Carettics reside in the southwestern peninsula of Wudor split into several polities, some of which were constituent states within the [[First Shining Empire]] and are thus under [[Teurechti People]] dominion. Other portions, in particular the eastern marches, lay under the control of the [[Kingdom of Valens]].
