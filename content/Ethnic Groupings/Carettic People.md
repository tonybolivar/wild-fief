> A Carettic Pirate.

Inhabitants of the "Birthplace of the Myriad" in the Ecumenicum, the Carettics reside in the southwestern peninsula of Wudor split into several polities, the majority of which are constituent states within the [[First Shining Empire]]. Other portions, in particular the eastern marches, lay under the control of the [[Kingdom of Valens]] and the [[Evershining Empire]].
